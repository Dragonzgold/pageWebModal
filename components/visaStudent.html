Student Visa I - 20

General Information

The United States welcomes foreign nationals coming to study in the United States. Prior to applying for a visa, all student visa applicants must be accepted and approved by their academic institution or program. Once accepted, educational institutions will provide each applicant with the necessary approval documentation to be submitted when applying for their student visa. Please note that a student visa cannot be issued earlier than 120 days before the program start date. You will not be interviewed for a student visa if you attend the Embassy prior to 120 days from the program start date. This school is authorized under federal law to enroll non-immigrant students.

Requirements to apply for the Student Visa program

  1. Valid Passport
  2. High School Diploma
  3. Minimum age: 16 years old

Student Visa Process

  1. Fill out your Student Visa application and send copies of your valid passport, high school diploma and birth certificate.
  2. Choose the Program of Study
    • Cosmetology - 1200 hours
    • Facial Esthetics - 260 hours
  3. Choose the start date of your classes (we start classes every week).
  4. Provide information about the Financial Sponsor who will be responsible for your housing, food and study expenses in the United States during your studies.
  5. Pay the entrance fee to ASM according to the program chosen to study:
    • a. Cosmetology - 1200 hours
    • b. Facial Esthetics - 260 hours
  6. The deposit must be made directly to the U.S. Bank in the form of an international wire transfer.
  7. You will receive your letter of acceptance from ASM as well as the original I-20 (M-1) form to be submitted to the American Embassy in your home country.
  8. Schedule your M-1 Student Visa appointment at the U.S. Embassy in your home country.
  9. After the interview at the American Embassy and if approved then you will be able to travel to the United States. The closest international airport to ASM is Fort Lauderdale, Florida or Miami, Florida.
