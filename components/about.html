About us

ASM Beauty World Academy - International is a bilingual (English and Spanish) beauty academy headquartered in Florida, USA; with over 35 years of experience in this industry. It offers continuing education in hairdressing, esthetics, nail technology, massage, makeup, electrolysis and body treatments to beauty professionals around the world interested in learning about new international trends and perfecting their skills in these areas.

We process homologation of studies and are approved by U.S. Federal Law to enroll Non-Immigrant Students through the I-20 (M-1) Student Visa program at our Florida, USA location.

Our Mission

To provide an exclusive, privileged and high-level education to our students in order to help them achieve their professional goals..

Our Vision

To leave a legacy in each of our students, providing them with an education based on principles of honesty, excellence and passion for the world of beauty.

Our Fundador

Salvatore “Sal” Milazzo, began his career in Sicily, Italy, where he studied hairdressing. After his studies, he arrived by ship to the United States, to New York, in the winter of 1964, and after several weeks in New York, he moved to Kansas City. In 1965, he opened his first salon: "L'Art Coiffure". In 1979, he moved to Florida, where he realized there was a need for a cosmetology school, which could provide training and education for people interested in becoming successful cosmetologists. In 1987, Mr. Milazzo, along with Ms. Tortora, founded ASM Beauty World Academy, Inc. in the city of Hollywood, Florida. Their commitment was to provide accessible education and training to all students but, in particular, to those coming from foreign countries. More than 35 years later, the commitment is as strong as the first day the school opened; today her legacy remains for each and every person who makes life at ASM Beauty World Academy..

Our President

Leticia Ramirez Medina, was born in Mexico City, he obtained his high school diploma at the "Instituto Renacimiento" in Mexico City. Upon graduation, she studied Tourism Business Administration. She moved to Miami on April 19, 1989, and married Mr. Sal V. Milazzo on December 28, 1992. She is now Mrs. Leticia Milazzo, owner and president of ASM Beauty World Academy, Inc. Mrs. Milazzo knows that education is the key to success and, therefore, has worked with World Champion and High Class Stylists from Europe and countries around the world. She has managed ASM Beauty World Academy since 1999. Ms. Milazzo felt the need to continue the legacy of ASM Beauty World Academy International in 2014, dedicating all her effort and passion for the world of beauty, to bring high level education to all those interested in this industry around the world, so that they have the opportunity to learn from internationally recognized speakers..
